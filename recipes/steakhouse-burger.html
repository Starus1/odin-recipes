Steakhouse Burger

A juicy steakhouse burger

This recipe features a tasty homemade steakhouse burger much like those you'd find at your local five-star steakhouse.

Ingredients

  • 20 oz dry-aged ribeye steak, ground
  • 20 oz brisket, ground
  • kosher salt and freshly ground white pepper, to taste
  • 4 sliced Muenster cheese
  • 4 homemade hamburger buns, sliced
  • Reserved caramelized onions

Steps

  1. Combine the ground ribeye and brisket in a large bowl.
  2. Form four 10 oz patties.
  3. Preheat the grill over high heat and just before adding the patties, season them generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the patties to the grill and cook on one side until well browned. Flip the burgers and add 1 slice of muenster cheese to each patty. Cook until well browned on the other side and the burgers have reached your desired doneness.
  5. Meanwhile, add the buns to the grill and cook until well toasted, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Remove the burgers and buns from the grill.
  7. Build the burgers and spread the caramelized onions on the top bun of each burger.
  8. Serve immediately.
