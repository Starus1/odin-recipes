French Herb Omlette

A caviar breakfast sandwich

This recipe features a classic European breakfast staple. A fishy yet savoury dish.

Ingredients

  • red caviar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • sourdough bread, sliced

Steps

  1. Prepare your sliced sourdough bread.
  2. Spread unsalted butter on your slices of bread.
  3. Use a spoon to gently scoop up caviar and spread desired amount above your buttered bread.
  4. Your traditional caviar breakfast sandwiches are complete! Enjoy!
